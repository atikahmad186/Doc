INDUSTRY COLLABORATIVE PROGRAM OF DIGITAL MARKETING

BBA- Specialised in Digital Marketing & Sales

Marketing has gone leap and bounces and crossed the traditional way of marketing products towards digital media for marketing goods. Emergence of e-commerce
industry and logistic companies serves as best infrastructure to support the Digital industry. The report, 2018 Marketing Hiring Trends, says 69% of companies
planned to hire more marketers in 2018. The most in-demand expertise were digital advertising (45%), content creation and curation (42%) and content strategy (39%).

crafted as per industry demands. While designing program, Industry requirement has been taken into consideration and to check the quality of program various
certification program has been added, such as Google, Facebook Blueprint and Hubspot.
